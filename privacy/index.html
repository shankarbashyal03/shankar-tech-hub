Privacy Policy

🔒 Our Commitment to Privacy

At ShankarTech Hub, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. All our tools run entirely in your browser. We do not require accounts, logins, or any form of registration.

  • No names — We don't ask for your name
  • No emails — We don't collect email addresses
  • No phone numbers — We don't request contact details
  • No location data — We don't track your location

💾 Data Storage

All data created using our tools is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your files and data never leave your device
  • We have no access to your documents
  • Your data is automatically cleared when you clear your browser data

🍪 Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for essential functions:

  • Theme preference — Dark/Light mode setting
  • Pinned tools — Your favorite tools list
  • PWA cache — For offline functionality

We do NOT use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

🔐 Third-Party Services

Some of our paid services use Google Forms for data collection. When you use these services:

  • You are redirected to Google Forms
  • Google's privacy policy applies to data submitted through forms
  • We receive only the information you choose to submit

🛡️ Security

Since all tools run client-side in your browser:

  • There is no server to hack
  • There is no database to breach
  • Your data is as secure as your device

👶 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
